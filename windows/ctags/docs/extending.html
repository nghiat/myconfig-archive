Extending ctagsÂ¶

Exuberant-ctags allows a user adding a new parser to ctags with --langdef=<LANG> and --regex-<LANG>=... options,

Universal-ctags follows and extends the design of Exuberant-ctags.

Universal-ctags encourages users to share the new parsers defined by the options. See optlib to know how you can share your parser definition with other.

Above new feature is very experimental, especially the directory layout for optlib <optlib> will be changed in the future.
